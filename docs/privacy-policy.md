# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 30, 2026

Jarvis AI Assistant is a voice-first AI assistant that lets users ask questions by voice or text and receive AI-generated responses.

## Information Processed

The app may process:

- Voice input converted to text by the browser or device speech recognition feature
- Text prompts typed by the user
- Conversation messages needed to generate assistant replies
- Basic server logs needed to operate and secure the backend

## How Information Is Used

Information is used to:

- Generate AI assistant responses
- Provide voice and text assistant functionality
- Maintain and troubleshoot the service

## Third-Party Processing

User prompts and conversation context may be sent from the backend to the configured AI provider to generate responses. API keys are stored on the backend and are not embedded in the mobile app.

## Data Retention

The app does not intentionally store long-term conversation history in the mobile app. Backend hosting providers and AI providers may retain logs or request data according to their own policies and the configuration of the deployed service.

## User Choices

Users can avoid voice input by typing messages instead. Users can revoke microphone permission from device or browser settings.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app developer.
