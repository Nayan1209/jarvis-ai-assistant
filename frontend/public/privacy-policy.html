Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 30, 2026

Jarvis AI Assistant is a voice-first AI assistant that lets users ask questions by voice or text and receive AI-generated responses.

Information Processed

The app may process voice input converted to text, typed prompts, conversation messages needed to generate replies, and basic server logs needed to operate and secure the backend.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to generate AI assistant responses, provide voice and text assistant functionality, and maintain or troubleshoot the service.

Third-Party Processing

User prompts and conversation context may be sent from the backend to the configured AI provider to generate responses. API keys are stored on the backend and are not embedded in the app.

User Choices

Users can avoid voice input by typing messages instead. Users can revoke microphone permission from device or browser settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app developer.
